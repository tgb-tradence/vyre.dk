### 1. Title: Animated Product Showcases

**Description**: This feature will use micro-animations and video elements to dynamically showcase the features and benefits of VYRE.DK hair dryers. On product pages, hovering over the image or scrolling down will trigger animations highlighting key features like ionic technology, intelligent heat control, or the high-speed motor. For example, an animation might show how the ionic technology smooths hair or how the heat sensor adjusts temperature in real-time. We can also incorporate short looping videos showcasing the dryer in use and the resulting style.

**Why**:
*   **Increased Engagement:** Striking visuals and interactive elements capture user attention and encourage exploration.
*   **Enhanced Product Understanding:** Animations effectively demonstrate complex technologies and their benefits, which are difficult to convey with static images or text alone. This feature is particularly impactful because we're selling premium technology.
*   **Improved Conversion Rates:** Clearer product understanding and increased engagement lead to higher confidence and a greater likelihood of purchase.
*   The research suggests using this approach to enhance UX and showcase products dynamically.

**Implementation**:
*   Utilize a combination of high-quality product photography and videography.
*   Employ a Javascript library like GreenSock (GSAP) or Framer Motion within React components to create smooth, engaging animations. Consider a Shopify app that specializes in adding video elements or animation if budget allows.

### 2. Title: Personalized Hair Profile & Recommendations

**Description**: Integrate a "Hair Profile" quiz or interactive form into the website. Customers answer questions about their hair type (straight, wavy, curly, coily), texture (fine, medium, coarse), concerns (frizz, damage, dryness), and styling goals. Based on their answers, the system recommends the most suitable VYRE.DK hair dryer and accessories. This recommendation can be accompanied by targeted styling tips and product usage guides.

**Why**:
*   **Improved User Experience:** Personalized recommendations make the shopping experience more relevant and efficient. Users feel understood and catered to, leading to increased satisfaction.
*   **Increased Conversion Rates:** Matching customers with the right product addresses their specific needs, boosting purchase confidence.
*   **Higher Average Order Value:** Recommending complementary accessories (e.g., diffusers, styling brushes) alongside the hair dryer increases the average transaction value.
*   **Reduced Returns:** By helping customers choose the right product from the start, we reduce the likelihood of returns due to dissatisfaction.

**Implementation**:
*   Develop a custom Shopify app or integrate a third-party quiz/recommendation engine (e.g., Typeform, Octane AI) via API.
*   The recommendation algorithm should consider product specifications (e.g., heat settings, attachments, ionic technology) and user-provided hair profile data.

### 3. Title: Sleek Dark Mode Toggle

**Description**: Implement a site-wide dark mode toggle, allowing users to switch between a light and dark color scheme. The dark mode design will be clean and minimal, adhering to modern e-commerce trends. It will feature high contrast for readability and will ensure all product images and videos look compelling in both modes.

**Why**:
*   **Enhanced Accessibility:** Dark mode can be easier on the eyes, particularly in low-light conditions, making the website more accessible to a wider audience.
*   **Modern Aesthetic:** Dark mode is a popular design trend, giving the website a sleek, contemporary feel that aligns with VYRE.DK's premium brand image.
*   **Improved User Experience:** Offering a choice of color schemes allows users to customize their browsing experience, increasing overall satisfaction.
*   **Differentiation:** Implementing dark mode will make our site stand out from competitors who have not adopted it yet.

**Implementation**:
*   Utilize CSS variables and media queries to define light and dark mode styles.
*   Implement a simple Javascript toggle button to switch between the two modes.
*   Ensure all images and videos are optimized for both light and dark backgrounds. This may involve creating alternative versions of certain assets. Consider using a pre-built Shopify theme that supports dark mode or offers easier customization options.
